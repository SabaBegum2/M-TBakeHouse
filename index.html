Meet the Heart Behind Mt Bakery

Hi! I'm Majeda Hussain, the founder of Mt Bakery. My love for baking started at home, where I found joy in creating desserts for my siblings especially during the long, quiet days of Covid. What began as a fun pastime soon turned into a real passion.

Determined to turn that passion into something meaningful, I enrolled in a professional baking program and had the incredible opportunity to learn from seasoned pastry chefs. From cookies to custom cakes, I now create a wide variety of sweet treats that bring people together and make moments unforgettable.

At Mt Bakery, every dessert is made with care, creativity, and a sprinkle of love. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, engagement, anniversary, or just want something sweet, we’re here to make it extra special.
